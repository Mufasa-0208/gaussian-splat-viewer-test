Iframe & URL

You can embed this viewer in another site using an <iframe> and pass a model URL: 

<iframe
  src="https://your-domain/splat-viewer/?model=https%3A%2F%2Fexample.com%2Fmodel.ply"
  width="800"
  height="600"
  frameborder="0"
></iframe>
